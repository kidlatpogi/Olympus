The Height of the Eiffel Tower

Did you know the Eiffel Tower can grow taller during the summer? Due to thermal expansion, the iron structure can expand by up to 15 centimeters (6 inches) when the weather gets hot.

Octopus Hearts

An octopus has three hearts. Two hearts pump blood to the gills, while the third circulates it through the rest of the body. Interestingly, the main circulatory heart stops beating when the octopus swims!
