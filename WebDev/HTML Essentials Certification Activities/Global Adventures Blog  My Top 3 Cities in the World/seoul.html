A Synthesis of History

Seoul matches rapid tech infrastructure with perfectly preserved history. One afternoon we were exploring the quiet, colorful courtyard structures of Gyeongbokgung Palace, and the next we were looking down at the city from the sky-high observation decks of the Lotte World Tower.

Late Night Neighborhoods

The energy level of this city doubles when the sun drops. We navigated the winding market paths of Myeongdong to track down spicy Korean Fried Chicken, sweet Hotteok pancakes, and toasted glasses of local refreshments under bright street market banners.

Personal Reflections

Crew Log: Hiking up along the ancient fortress walls of Mount Namsan right at sunset gave us a view of an endless sea of residential and office glass towers glowing blue and gold into the horizon.
