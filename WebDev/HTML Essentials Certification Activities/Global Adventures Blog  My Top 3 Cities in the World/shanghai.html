The Architectural Continuum

Standing along The Bund looking straight out at Lujiazui feels like peeking directly into the next century. The twisting, soaring silhouette of the Shanghai Tower cuts effortlessly through the low clouds, standing tall directly beside colonial European facades that flank the western pedestrian promenade.

Culinary Discoveries

Nothing brings our travel group together quite like a fresh tray of hot, piping steaming Shengjianbao (pan-fried pork soup dumplings). Crispy on the bottom, incredibly savory inside, we ate them on simple plastic stools on backstreets behind the Yu Garden bazaar.

Personal Reflections

Crew Log: Waking up exceptionally early at 5:30 AM to walk along the riverfront was magical. Watching groups cleanly practice Tai Chi against a foggy backdrop of skyscrapers remains etched in our travel diaries.
