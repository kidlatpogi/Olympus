Landmarks & Distinct Urban Neighborhoods

From the towering apex of the Tokyo Skytree down to the ancient wooden gates of Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa, Tokyo keeps you constantly guessing. Walking across the historic Shibuya Crossing with thousands of paths crossing simultaneously is a sensory rush quite unlike anything else on Earth.

Culinary Profiles

Our journey revolved heavily around food. We found true gastronomic bliss inside narrow subterranean ramen shops in Tokyo Station, savored delicately balanced fatty tuna pieces at Tsukiji Outer Market, and shared endless plates of sizzling yakitori skewers down memory-filled alleyways.

Personal Reflections

Crew Log: Getting turned around searching for a hidden vinyl listening bar in Shinjuku during a light drizzle remains our absolute favorite memory. We found it, listened to legendary jazz records, and watched trains click across the tracks below all evening.
